Identity Crime

These are the wanted individuals who have committed an act/acts of identity crime. Information about each has been provided. If you have information or have seen any of these suspects, please contact us.

Notice: The list of identity criminals is currently being expanded. Please check back here regularly for updates.

Felix Osilama Okpoh

Felix Osilama Okpoh is wanted for his alleged involvement in an identity crime scheme that defrauded over 70 different businesses in the United States resulting in combined losses of over $6,000,000 USD. He has close associations with cybercriminal Richard Izuchukwu Uzuh.

Uxbridge Fraud Group

Not operating by a known name, this group has been a recent finding and has been committing numerous acts of identity crime, and in turn been using stolen identities to commit further acts of fraud against Uxbridge and Hillingdon citizens.

Yousef Kurdy

Yousef Kurdy is wanted for allegedly seperately claiming the identity of more than 35,000 office visits with NHS patients, submitting hundreds of fraudulent claims when office visits never took place, billing for treating patients during times when he was not present, and failing to pay taxes for three years.

Marshall Chester Cash

Marshall Chester Cash is wanted for identity theft of thousands of younger students in the Borough, and the misuse of said information for purposes of fraud.
