Intellectual Property

These are the wanted individuals who have committed an act/acts of intellectual property crime. Information about each has been provided. If you have information or have seen any of these suspects, please contact us.

Notice: The list of intellectual property criminals is currently being expanded. Please check back here regularly for updates.

Nikolay Almazov Krechet

Nikolay Almazov Krechet is wanted for his participation in a stolen IP scheme. From at least 2014 to August of 2015, Krechet was the leader of an organization that engaged in the repeated violation of copyright law. Krechet stole and sold millions of pounds worth of software and music.

John Antony Porcaro

John Anthony Porcaro is wanted for his alleged involvement in a counterfeiting scheme which defrauded more than 400 individuals throughout the Borough of Hillingdon out of more than £5 million. Between February and December of 1998, the groups were doing business as distributors of counterfeited goods and repeatedly violated copyright law.

Phet Loi Naovarath

Phet Loi Naovarath is wanted for his alleged participation in the illegal and distribution of patents stolen from a public UK database.

Marshall Chester Cash

Marshall Chester Cash is wanted for his involvement in the development of a torrent distribution website. The website, named 'BananaTorrents', is responsible for distribution of illegally-obtained software, music, and movies, it's reported 'sales' resulting in at least £50 million to both UK and worldwide companies and organisations.
