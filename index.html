What is Uxbridge Most Wanted?

Uxbridge Most Wanted is an initiative by the London Borough of Hillingdon aiming to bring criminals in the Uxbridge area to justice. It allows members of the public to play a crucial role in keeping their community safe by working alongside each other and organiations such as the Borough and the Metropolitan Police.

By participating, you will be reducing and preventing crime in the area (and likely other areas as well), and keeping fellow citizens safer. Participation comes at no cost to you besides a few minutes of your time on a regular basis to check the website or report something to us.

How do I participate?

Participation is simple and easy.

  1. Inform yourself on current affairs by frequently visiting the News section below. This will keep you up to date on matters this website covers.
  2. Learn about presently-wanted criminals in the area by visiting the Suspects pages. This will be highly helpful to the Police as you may spot any of them, and in which case you should follow the next point.
  3. Report any concerns, sightings, or crimes, or ask about anything related to this website by contacting us on the Contact Us page.

News

Uxbridge Most Wanted Launched

The Uxbridge Most Wanted; is a new initiative by Hillingdon Borough to publicise and promote awareness of people who are most wanted within the borough. The overall goal of this website is to have citizens play their part in ensuring that their neighbourhood is safe and free of crime, and the way of doing so is by raising awareness of these wanted individuals and providing citizens of the area a method of reporting them as well as finding information about them.

This new initiative should, providing you do your part in participation, keep the community safer.

If you are interested in participating, please refer to the instructions earlier in the page. It's a simple process that needn't take more than five minutes or so of your time, and only requires you to refer back to this website once a week or so.

We, the London Borough of Hillingdon, hope this initiative succeeds and makes yours and others' lives better, through everyone's participation in ensuring the community remains as safe as it can be.
